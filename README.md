Sayaca is an adventure and exploration video game where the sacred masks that maintain the world's balance are in danger. A technocratic order attempts to exploit them for its own interests, ignoring their limitations and consequences. Quimey must recover them before their power is used to irreversibly alter nature and humanity. The player experiences two sensations: calm and excitement. The game features level-based gameplay, each telling a story and featuring thrilling combat.
